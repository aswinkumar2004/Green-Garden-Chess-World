Structured Curriculum

At Green Garden Chess World, we take pride in our well-structured curriculum that guides players through their learning journey. Our program is designed to nurture growth step-by-step and enhance players' understanding of the game at every level.

  1. Beginner Level: Introduction to the board, piece movements, and basic rules.
  2. Intermediate Level: Core strategies, common openings, and developing tactical skills.
  3. Advanced Level: Complex endgames, advanced openings, and analyzing grandmaster games.
  4. Mastery Workshops: Specialized lessons focusing on deep strategic concepts and tournament preparation.

* Our curriculum is curated by seasoned chess experts who blend theory and practice, ensuring that players build strong foundations and progress confidently. Join us and start your journey towards becoming a chess master!

* A well-organised curriculum with monthly competitions, access to an educational portal, progress reports and consistent homework and assignments.

* A well-organised curriculum with a two-hour weekly schedule across two days.

* Learning is effective and enjoyable with our friendly trained FIDE-rated Instructors.
